Chilaqulies de Pollo, Huevo y Picante

Ingredients

  • 6-8 corn tortillas
  • 3/4 cup shredded cooked chicken
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 chili
  • 1/4 tsp. cumin
  • 1/4 tsp. oregano
  • Grated cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • sour cream for serving
  • cilantro to serve
  • lemon to serve

Steps

  1. Fry the corn tortillas and set aside.
  2. In a blender add the tomato sauce, onion, garlic, chili, cumin, oregano and season with salt and pepper. Mingle.
  3. In a pan on the fire add 2 tbsp. of olive oil and then the preparation. Cook for approximately 8-10 minutes
  4. Then add the already cooked chicken and mix.
  5. With the fire off, integrate the fried tortillas.
  6. Prepare a separate fried egg.
  7. To serve add the fried egg and top with grated cheese, sour cream, cilantro and a little lemon.
